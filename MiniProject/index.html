Saving Lives Through Donation

Your one donation can save multiple lives. Be a hero today — donate blood or register as an organ donor.

Looking for Blood Looking for Organ Donate

Who Can Donate Blood?

✔ Individuals aged 18–65 years (some places allow 17 with guardian consent).
✔ Minimum weight approximately 50 kg (rules vary).
✔ Hemoglobin within healthy range and free from infections such as HIV/Hepatitis.
✔ Not currently suffering from major chronic disease, recent surgery, or recent major travel/illness as per local guidelines.

One blood donation can save up to 3 lives.

Who Can Donate Organs?

✔ Living donors: healthy persons can donate one kidney or part of liver after medical evaluation and consent.
✔ Deceased donation: organs like heart, lungs, liver, pancreas and corneas may be donated after brain death with legal consent.
✔ Donation requires medical compatibility checks and legal consent to ensure safety and ethics.

Why Donation is Important

Blood and organ donation gives a second chance to people with accidents, organ failure, or life-threatening conditions. A single organ donor can save multiple lives and blood donors help patients in surgeries, trauma care, and chronic conditions.

Donation is a gift of life — consider registering and spreading awareness.
